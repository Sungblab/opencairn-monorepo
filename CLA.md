Agreement by **either**:

that reads:

    Signed-off-by: Your Real Name <your-email@example.com>

The presence of the `OpenCairn-CLA: accepted v1.0` trailer indicates explicit
acceptance of this Agreement, in addition to the Developer Certificate of
Origin attestation conveyed by `Signed-off-by`.

the OpenCairn Contributor License Agreement v1.0."*

Contributions (typo fixes, minor doc edits, small dependency bumps).
